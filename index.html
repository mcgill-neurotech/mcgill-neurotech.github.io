The Thinking Cap

Take a look at our Github for our award-winning solution called "The Thinking Cap". It is a truly adaptive learning analysis system for accelerated and extended flow states. It leverages neural networks to train on personalized intricacies of your EEG and helps you learn better habits so that you can create your Pieta.
